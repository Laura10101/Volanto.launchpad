The Launchpad Programme

Who is it for?

The Launchpad Programme is for anyone who wants to start a career in tech, or progress their existing tech career. You might be:

  • A bootcamp or uni graduate struggling to secure your first role
  • A career changer with experience in another industry but no idea where to start within the tech industry
  • A developer looking to make the leap to architect or Head of Engineering
Technology needs diversity. It needs a variety of people, from a range of backgrounds and experiences.

What does it do?

The Launchpad Programme is a 3 month programme. You will be paired with a senior technologist and have one-to-one sessions with your mentor to explore your career goals, consider what direction might be best for you, and help you get there. Advice will include:

  • CV review and job application guidance
  • Training and skills suggestions - perhaps there is a particular language or framework you should focus on for your career choice
  • Job seeking preparation and interview advice
  • The benefit of your mentors network

What is expected from you?

A career in tech is not easy. It requires ongoing learning and a huge amount of commitment and dedication. This programme is no different. You will need to be committed to investing time and energy into your future.

Who is eligible?

In order to be eligible for this programme, you must be:

  • Based in the UK
  • Committed to developing a successful career in tech
We welcome candidates of any age and background.
